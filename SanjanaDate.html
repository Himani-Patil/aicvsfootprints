Bringing Realism in Mobile Augmented Reality

By Sanjana Date

As A part of AICVS Webinar 3.0, AICVS invited Sanjana Date, A Btech student at Cummins College Of Engineering for Women to share her knowledge and guide the audience through the domain of ‘Bringing Realism in Mobile Augmented Reality’.

Augmented reality Vs Virtual reality

To kick off the session, Sanjana gave a brief introduction about Augmented reality explaining the difference between the Augmented reality (AR augments your surroundings by adding digital elements to a live view, often by using the camera on a smartphone), Virtual reality (VR is a completely immersive experience that replaces a real-life environment with a simulated one) and mixed reality(Combines real and virtual reality).

Further She talked about several AR Concepts including occlusion (the ability to cover virtual AR objects with objects physically located in the room) and Pose in 3D. She also talked about various technological advancements such as Learning Optimized Local Difference Binaries, Space Sharing Using Markerless AR and AR Core by google.

Next Sanjana Talked about Interaction in MAR Precision Target Selection using the following techniques:

Techniques

  • PR-Cone-Casting
  • Offset-Ray-Casting
  • 2D-Grid-Cursor
  • PR-Bubble-cursor

Designing for MAR

  • 2D interactions
  • Environment Consideration
  • Mental models
  • Visual affordance

Mar Application Examples

  • Ikea Place
  • Flipkart Camera

AR Platforms

  • Unity
  • Unreal
  • AR Core
  • ARKit
  • Snap AR

Sanjana also gave an interactive demo demonstrating Mobile Augmented reality making the session even more exciting and insightful.

In conclusion, Sanjana discussed about the growing market, The enhanced user Experience, the applications and the ongoing research around augmented reality leaving the audience excited to dive deeper into the world of the same!
