Data analytics Competition: Latest Covid-19 India state-wise dataset

AICVS held another beginner friendly Kaggle competition from 5th December 2021 to 11th December 2021. Coronaviruses are a large family of viruses which may cause illness in animals or humans. In humans, several coronaviruses are known to cause respiratory infections ranging from the common cold to more severe diseases such as Middle East Respiratory Syndrome (MERS) and Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome (SARS). The most recently discovered coronavirus causes coronavirus disease COVID-19 - World Health Organization.

This dataset contains latest Covid-19 India state-wise data as on November 28, 2021. It consists of 9 columns representing the attributes and 36 data entries. Your task is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis on this dataset and draft a report to summarize your findings. Provide as many meaningful inferences using graphs and charts as you can. You are allowed to use any data visualization library or tools.

The winners of the competition were:

  • Ananya Joshi
  • Isha Shukla
  • Yadnya Vyavahare
  • Shriya Deshpande

The participants who made submissions were given a Completion Certificate and the winners got a Certificate of Excellence.

We would like to thank the participants for showing so much vigor and enthusiasm for the Kaggle competitions hosted by us. We hope to bring you many more competitions like this.
